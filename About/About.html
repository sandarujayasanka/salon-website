Our salon services are designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Our salon product range is designed not only to meet your beauty needs but also to enhance your overall look with modern style. Welcome to Bhagya Salon Private Limited, your ultimate destination for
beauty and elegance. Since our inception on April 17th, 2017,we have
been dedicated to providing exceptional beauty services that enhance your natural charm and boost your confidence.
Our journey from Highlevel Road, Nugegoda, to our new luxurious
location at Idh Road, Kotikawatta, reflects our commitment to
excellence and growth. At Bhagya Salon, we believe in the
transformative power of beauty. Our team of skilled professionals is
passionate about delivering personalized care using the latest
techniques and top-quality products. We strive to create an
environment where you can relax, rejuvenate, and leave feeling your
best. I am incredibly proud of what we have achieved and grateful for the trust and support of our clients. We are excited to continue our journey with you, offering innovative services and unparalleled experiences. Thank you for choosing Bhagya Salon. We look forward to welcoming you and making your beauty dreams come true.

Our Vision and
Mission

Our vision is to set the standard for beauty and wellness in Sri Lanka by combining cutting-edge techniques with a personalized touch. We are dedicated to continually improving our services and facilities to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our mission is to provide exceptional beauty services that enhance the natural beauty of our clients. We strive to create a welcoming and relaxing environment where every client feels valued and pampered.
